Data visualization is a powerful tool that transforms raw data into meaningful insights through interactive and visually appealing representations. It enhances our ability to understand complex datasets and make informed decisions.
As part of my learning journey, I created a comprehensive and interactive Renewable Energy Dashboard. 

## This dashboard showcases:
- 📊 Renewable Capacity (MW): Tracking the growth of hydropower, solar energy, wind energy, and other renewables from 2000 to 2019.
- 🌍 Global Consumption: Visualizing the shift in energy sources over the decades.
- 💼 Jobs in Renewable Energy: Highlighting employment trends in various renewable sectors.
- 💰 Top 5 for Investment: Presenting investment trends across different countries in renewable energy.
- 🌏 Regional Focus: Examining renewable capacity across different regions and countries.

This interactive dashboard utilizes slicers, pivot tables, charts, infographics, and hyperlinking to provide a dynamic and engaging user experience. Feel free to explore it and see how data visualization can bring data to life!
